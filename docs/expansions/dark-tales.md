# Expansion 5 - Dark Tales: The Cursed Storybook

## Theme And Visual Identity

Dark Tales is a parody fairy-tale set inspired by princess-adjacent archetypes without using protected names. It turns glass slippers, thorn castles, sea bargains, poisoned mirrors, tower braids, winter courts, beastly manors, lantern festivals, and clockwork midnight into adult gothic glamour.

Visual anchors: moonlit castles, storybook frames with no readable text, black roses, pearl foam, poisoned apples, glass, satin gowns, thorn halos, gilded cages, candlelit libraries, and midnight blue magic. All characters are adult-coded.

## Mechanic Summary

- **Cycling**: early smoothing and graveyard setup.
- **Flashback**: storybook spells returning for a second telling.
- Primary colors: U/B/W, with R rebellious heroines and G cursed forests.
- Mechanical identity: value-control, graveyard spell recursion, hand smoothing, and long-game inevitability.

## Precon Identity

**Midnight Storybook** - U/B/W value-control. The deck cycles early, fills the graveyard, and wins by replaying efficient flashback spells while stabilizing with lifegain and tap effects.

## Gauntlet Boss Concepts

- **Glass-Coffin Queen** - W/B grind boss with flashback value, lifelink, and poison-mirror removal.
- **Abyssal Songstress** - U/B tempo-control boss with cycling payoff, tap effects, and sea bargain card advantage.

## Art-Bible Follow-Up Notes

- Use original parody naming and silhouettes; avoid direct protected character names.
- Storybook pages, invitations, mirrors, and labels must be blank or decorative with no readable text.
- Keep the tone glamorous and adult, not cute or juvenile.
