# Expansion 2 - Celtic Fae: The Silver Veil

Linked block: **Celtic Fae** is part one of the Celtic/Arthurian two-set block. It introduces the Otherworld, fate manipulation, exile pressure, and scry setup. Arthurian Court follows as the mortal response to the bargains made here.

## Theme And Visual Identity

Celtic Fae is a silver-green twilight set built around fae courts, war omens, selkies, banshees, sidhe duelists, old hill gates, moonlit bogs, and impossible bargains. The set should feel elegant, dangerous, and transactional: every gift has a hook.

Visual anchors: silver moonlight, moss green, blackthorn, mist, pale gold torcs, raven feathers, glassy pools, mushroom rings, thorn crowns, and hollow hills. All characters are adult-coded.

## Mechanic Summary

- **Exile**: banishment, temporary removal, graveyard hate, and "lost beyond the veil" effects.
- **Scry**: fate-reading, omen setup, and consistency for slower control shells.
- Primary colors: U/B/G, with W fae knights and R wild hunt pressure.
- Mechanical identity: tempo-control, topdeck control, evasive fae, exile-based answers, and scry payoff cards.

## Precon Identity

**Glimmer Bargain** - U/B/G tempo-control. The deck plays evasive fae, scry smoothing, selective exile answers, and value creatures that turn topdeck control into steady pressure.

## Gauntlet Boss Concepts

- **The Morrigan, Black-Wing Omen** - B/G exile-control boss. Plays removal, graveyard hate, evasive threats, and late-game inevitability.
- **Titania of the Silver Court** - U/G fae-token boss. Plays scry engines, untouchable threats, and wide fae pressure.

## Art-Bible Follow-Up Notes

- Keep all fae adult-coded and regal rather than childlike.
- Avoid readable ogham, runes, or text on stones, banners, cards, mirrors, and books.
- Establish a consistent fae silhouette language: elongated cloaks, thorn crowns, silver jewelry, moonlit eyes, and nature-integrated armor.
- Celtic + Arthurian should share visual DNA but differ in texture: fae = mist/silver/thorn; Arthurian = steel/grail/banner/castle.
