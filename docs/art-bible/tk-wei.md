# Darling Blades Art Bible — Wei (`tk-wei`)

Wei is the disciplined, ambitious north: officers who win by preparation, doctrine,
and cold nerve. The faction color story is **lapis + bronze** — deep imperial blue
lacquer, brushed bronze plate, and pale ivory silk — layered on each card's
color-identity anchor (W/U/B for administrators and champions, R for the fast
Xiahou cavalry). Architecture is **rammed-earth ramparts, watchtowers, and
banner-lined command tents**; the recurring insignia is the Wei tiger-and-cloud
crest stamped in bronze. Costume language: structured shoulder plate over long
scholar-or-officer robes, high collars, campaign cloaks, disciplined topknots and
hairpins. Strategists read as unhurried and dangerous; warriors read as drilled,
not wild. Keep whites textured and darks deep — Wei glints, it does not glow. The
**Cao family** (`caocao`, `caoren`, `caopi`) shares ink-black hair, sharp amber
eyes, and an imperious brow, at rank-appropriate regalia richness.
