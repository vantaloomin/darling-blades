# Darling Blades Art Bible — Wu (The River Dynasty)

Wu is water and wildfire. Its women rule the Yangtze from lacquered war-junks and
riverside pavilions, so paint wet light: sun bouncing off green-brown current,
mist over reeds, the orange bloom of fireships on black water. The faction accents
are **crimson + river-gold** — vermilion sashes, cinnabar lacquer, and worn gilt on
scale armor, banners, and boat fittings. Costume language runs to layered silks over
light lamellar, high side-slits for river mobility, and tiger motifs threaded through
the ruling **Sun family** (registry: emerald eyes and tiger ornament recur). Strategists
lean U — teal robes, scrolls, cool study-light; admirals and Sun warlords lean R — ember,
smoke, and the reflected fire of Red Cliffs. Architecture is bamboo scaffold, junk rigging,
and low lacquered decks, never stone keeps. Keep water in the story wherever the band allows.
