# Darling Blades Art Bible — Gothic Monsters (`gm`)

Gothic Monsters: Nocturne Manor is candlelit horror-glamour: vampire courts,
stitched brides, wolf-cursed nobility, porcelain dolls, cathedral witches,
revenant aristocrats, storm towers, and grave gardens. The register is
luxurious, never grimy — crimson velvet, black lace, moonlit stone, stormglass,
cathedral gold, candle wax, iron gates, grave roses, white lightning, and old
manor interiors. Every subject is an adult-coded woman. Keep the set visually
distinct from Celtic Fae (no mist-and-thorn wilderness) and Arthurian Court (no
polished-steel white-gold daylight): Nocturne Manor is interior night, candle
warmth against storm cold. No readable labels anywhere — invitations, books,
gravestones, lab diagrams, sheet music, and chapel banners stay blank or
pictorial. Dreaded cards must read as hard to block: multiple silhouettes,
aggressive angles, or crowded compositions. Reserve a clear empty top third
above the tallest silhouette for smart-crop headroom on every card.
