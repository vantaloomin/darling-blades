# Darling Blades Art Bible — Greek Pantheon (`gk`)

The Olympians read as one civilization: sun-bleached Pentelic **marble** (`#f2e8cf`),
**Aegean blue** in sea, sky, and dyed wool, and **gilt** trim on every hem, cuff, and
laurel. Costume language is the draped chiton, himation, and peplos — pleated cloth
that catches rim light and holds its integrity in motion — over sandaled greaves and
worked-bronze accents. Architecture is fluted colonnade, coffered ceiling, and open
horizon; nature is olive, cypress, wine-dark sea. Each goddess owns one signature
attribute (aegis, thunderbolt, silver bow, poppy, trident) rendered as a real forged
or grown object, never a glowing abstraction. Goddesses appear as-is; gods are
genderbent but kit-faithful. The faction accents — marble, Aegean blue, gilt — layer
over each card's color-identity anchor and never replace it.
