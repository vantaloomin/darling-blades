# Darling Blades Art Bible — Other Officers (`tk-other`)

The coalition-era wildcards: tyrants, sorcerers, and the peerless warrior who
answers to no banner. Their unifying look is **black-iron and ember** — lacquered
dark armor, soot-darkened steel, and heat that leaks through the cracks. Where the
Three Kingdoms proper wear clean house colors, the Others wear scavenged grandeur:
mismatched loot-plate, torn court silk over war-leather, jade fiefs won by force.
Architecture is the burning capital and the frontier war-camp — Luoyang in flames,
canvas tents under a broken sky. Rendering runs hot and low-key: fire as light,
smoke as atmosphere, one cold rim to carve the silhouette out of the dark. These
are the era's opportunists and monsters, painted with the same reverence given to
kings, because for one violent season each of them held the world by the throat.
