# Darling Blades Art Bible — Jin (三晉)

Jin is the faction of the long game — patience weaponized, ambition worn thin as a
smile. Where Wei blazes and Wu roars, Jin waits in cold rooms and reads the ledger.
The visual key is **restraint that reads as threat**: still poses, controlled hands,
eyes that have already finished the argument. Recurring materials are **slate-teal
lacquer, tarnished silver filigree, oiled dark silk, and grey-lit stone** — administrative
architecture (archive halls, map rooms, screened corridors) over battlefield chaos.
Costume language runs high-collared, layered, and sleeve-heavy, hiding the hands until
they matter. The **slate-teal + tarnished silver** accents (section 4) manifest as cool
robe underlayers, oxidized clasps and hairpins, and a single silver highlight that never
warms. The Sima family (`simayi`, `simashi`, `simazhao`) share cool grey eyes, ash-dark
hair, and unreadable composure per the canon registry — Yi carries the silver streak her
sons echo. Faces stay bright and quiet; the shimmer lives in the silver, never the skin.
